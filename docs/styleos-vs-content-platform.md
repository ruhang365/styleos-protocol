# StyleOS vs Content Platform

StyleOS is different from content platforms such as short-video, social, or recommendation platforms.

## Content Platforms

Content platforms distribute content:

- posts
- videos
- live streams
- comments
- traffic
- recommendations
- creator-fan interaction

They are the traffic layer.

## StyleOS

StyleOS helps creators productize services:

- collect structured fan information
- write rule-backed advice
- generate lite reports
- deliver execution cards
- collect feedback
- improve service workflows

StyleOS is the workflow / protocol / service layer.

## Relationship

Creators can acquire attention on content platforms and deliver structured services through StyleOS-compatible workflows.
