# Why Creators First

StyleOS serves creators first because creators already have the real workflow problem.

## Creators Have Audiences

Creators receive styling, outfit, hairstyle, makeup, and image questions from real fans and clients.

## Creators Have Experience

Creators already make repeated judgments. StyleOS helps turn those judgments into structured, reviewable, reusable workflows.

## Creators Lack Tools

Many creators still rely on private messages, manual replies, screenshots, notes, and one-off reports.

They need:

- intake forms
- service menus
- lite report templates
- rule cards
- execution cards
- feedback tools

## Creators Can Contribute Rules and Cases

Creators can abstract repeated service patterns into privacy-safe rule cards and synthetic cases.

## Creators Are the First Real Small-B Users

Creator services are small-B workflows. They need practical delivery tools before the market needs a broad consumer app.
