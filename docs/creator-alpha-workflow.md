# Creator Alpha Workflow

This workflow describes how creators can join StyleOS Creator Alpha and contribute safely.

中文说明：
Creator Alpha 用于验证粉丝咨询标准化、Lite Report 交付、反馈收集和规则抽象。公开仓库不接收真实照片、联系方式或未经授权案例。

## 1. Creator Applies

The creator applies for Alpha through an official channel provided by ruhang365 or the project maintainers.

Creators may describe their segment, service type, current workflow, and what they want to test.

## 2. Creator Receives Intake Template

The creator receives the StyleOS fan intake template and service workflow instructions.

Relevant files:

- [Fan Intake Form](../creator-kit/fan-intake-form.md)
- [Creator Workflow](../creator-kit/creator-workflow.md)

## 3. Creator Collects Fan Info with Consent

The creator collects fan information privately with consent.

Raw fan photos, questionnaires, and private notes must remain outside the public repository.

## 4. Creator Tags Fan Profile

The creator uses StyleOS tags to structure the profile.

Relevant files:

- [Style Tag Schema](style-tag-schema.md)
- [Modules](../modules/README.md)

## 5. Creator Uses Lite Report Template

The creator uses the Lite Report template to draft a deliverable report.

Relevant files:

- [Report Schema](report-schema.md)
- [Report Lite Template](../creator-kit/report-lite-template.md)

## 6. Creator Delivers Report Manually

The creator reviews the report manually and delivers it through their own service channel.

AI output or template output should not be delivered without creator review.

## 7. Creator Collects Feedback

The creator collects feedback about clarity, actionability, usefulness, and execution.

Feedback should be stored privately unless it is anonymized and authorized for reuse.

## 8. Creator Submits Abstracted Rules / Schema Suggestions

The creator may submit:

- abstracted rule cards;
- schema suggestions;
- synthetic examples;
- workflow improvements;
- evaluation ideas.

No raw personal data should be submitted.

## 9. ruhang365 Reviews and Merges Suitable Content

Maintainers review contributions for:

- privacy safety;
- non-harmful language;
- protocol consistency;
- creator usefulness;
- commercial boundary clarity;
- trademark safety.

Suitable content may be merged into the open repository.

## 10. Validated Content May Enter StyleOS Pro Under Separate Terms

Some repeated, authorized, or expert-reviewed patterns may become candidates for StyleOS Pro.

This requires separate terms and does not happen automatically.

中文说明：
经过验证的内容可能在另行约定下进入 StyleOS Pro。小 B 工具产生的数据不会默认进入开源库或高级库。
