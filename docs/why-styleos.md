# Why StyleOS

Personal styling services are moving from individual experience toward standardization, tooling, and model-assisted workflows.

中文说明：
个人造型服务正在从“创作者个人经验 + 私聊回复 + 零散参考图”走向更结构化的形态。StyleOS 尝试提供一个开放协议，让创作者、小 B、开发者可以围绕同一套结构协作。

## The Problem

Styling advice is often valuable but hard to reuse:

- intake is inconsistent
- advice is repeated manually
- reports are hard to standardize
- execution notes are unclear
- feedback is not captured
- cases are sensitive and cannot be casually published

## The StyleOS Approach

StyleOS organizes the workflow into:

- structured inputs
- tags
- rule cards
- lite reports
- execution cards
- validation
- feedback loops

## Why This Matters

The market should not only have closed tests or closed apps. A shared protocol can help creators, developers, and small businesses build compatible tools and workflows.

StyleOS starts with open protocol documents and creator tools, then leaves room for future hosted products and Pro libraries under a clear open-core boundary.
