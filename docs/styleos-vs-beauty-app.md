# StyleOS vs Beauty App

StyleOS is different from beauty apps, AI retouching tools, and virtual makeup tools.

## Beauty Apps

Beauty apps usually optimize images:

- retouching
- filters
- virtual makeup
- face editing
- image enhancement
- instant previews

They are mostly consumer-facing tools.

## StyleOS

StyleOS structures styling decisions and services:

- what information to collect
- how to label styling signals
- how to write rule cards
- how to generate lite reports
- how to create execution cards
- how creators collect feedback

## Key Difference

Beauty apps change or preview an image.

StyleOS helps creators and small businesses deliver repeatable styling workflows.

中文说明：
StyleOS 不是美图、AI 修图或试妆工具。它不以优化图片为核心，而是以造型服务流程、规则卡、报告和执行沟通为核心。
