## Explanation

想显成熟专业时，厚重齐刘海可能让视觉更偏学生感。更稳妥的方式是增加露额秩序和整体清爽度。

## Execution Notes

- Brief: "Make bangs lighter or side-parted for a more professional impression."

## Limitations

- Some users intentionally want youthful softness.
- Must consider forehead exposure comfort.
