## Explanation

发量少时，过度打薄可能让支撑更弱。starter 策略应先保护支撑，再寻找低维护的蓬松方式。

## Execution Notes

- Barber brief: "Please avoid heavy thinning; I need support and light volume."
- Keep daily maintenance realistic.

## Limitations

- Does not address medical hair loss or scalp treatment.
- Fine hair and low density need separate review.
