## Explanation

圆润轮廓想显清爽时，重点通常是减少两侧堆积和提高轮廓秩序，而不是用厚刘海遮挡。

## Execution Notes

- Ask for "light face-framing, controlled side volume, and easy daily styling."
- Keep the tone neutral and avoid face-width judgment.

## Limitations

- Not suitable when the user prefers a very cute, heavy-bang style.
- Crown support should be moderate; too much height may conflict with other goals.
