## Explanation

发量多时，问题往往是重量和外扩位置，而不是长度本身。只剪短可能让体积更难控制。

## Execution Notes

- Ask for "weight reduction and cleaner outline, not just shorter length."
- Confirm maintenance time before adding layers.

## Limitations

- Curly or natural curl hair needs more specific professional review.
- Not suitable for users who want maximum volume.

## Privacy Note

Keep real salon photos private unless authorized and anonymized.
