## Explanation

额头偏高时，平衡不等于完全遮挡。轻刘海、侧分和碎发可以提供更可控的调整空间。

## Execution Notes

- Ask for "light, adjustable forehead balance."
- Mention whether the user wants to keep a professional or clean impression.

## Limitations

- Not suitable when hairline or scalp concerns require private professional consultation.
- Requires maintenance review if bangs need frequent trimming.

## Privacy Note

Do not expose real hairline photos publicly.
