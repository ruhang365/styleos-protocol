## Explanation

职场妆面不等于浓妆。稳定、清晰、不过度分散注意力，通常更符合专业沟通场景。

## Execution Notes

- Brief: clean base, ordered brows, soft definition, stable lip color.

## Limitations

- Stage, performance, or strong lighting contexts may need more intensity.
- Company culture can differ.
