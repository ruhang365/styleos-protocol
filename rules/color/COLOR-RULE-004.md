## Explanation

职场场景中，中性色通常比流行色更稳定。流行色可以作为小面积点缀，而不是默认主色。

## Execution Notes

- Shopping keywords: neutral base, business casual, stable color.

## Limitations

- Creative roles may need stronger color memory points.
- Company culture matters.
