## Required Boundaries

- Do not submit real user photos.
- Do not submit real names or contacts.
- Do not present starter rules as expert-certified.
- Do not use body-shaming, appearance-shaming, or deterministic language.
- Do not recommend cosmetic surgery, weight loss, or medical changes.
- Keep advice practical and scenario-aware.
