# v0.2 Launch Calendar

14-day launch rhythm for StyleOS Protocol v0.2.

## Day 1

- Update README.
- Create GitHub Release.
- Publish WeChat / Moments post.
- Share [RELEASE.md](../RELEASE.md).

## Day 2

- Publish GitHub / developer community post.
- Share protocol, schema, rule-card, and open-core positioning.
- Invite developers and PMs to Star.

## Day 3

- Privately message 10 creators.
- Prioritize outfit, hairstyle, image, makeup, and photography / styling creators.
- Track replies in the manual dashboard.

## Day 4

- Publish Xiaohongshu post.
- Focus on creator pain points and Alpha recruitment.

## Day 5

- Publish Jike post.
- Focus on startup, AI, open-source, open-core, and small-B tools.

## Day 6

- Collect FAQ feedback.
- Update [FAQ](../FAQ.md) if repeated questions appear.
- Clarify open-core and privacy boundaries.

## Day 7

- Review Stars, visits, private conversations, and Alpha applications.
- Identify top objections and strongest use cases.
