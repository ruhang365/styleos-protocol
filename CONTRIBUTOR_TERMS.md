# Contributor Terms

By submitting content to this repository through issues, pull requests, discussions, or other contribution channels, you confirm the terms below.

## Ownership and Rights

You confirm that:

- the contribution is original, or you have the right to contribute it;
- the contribution does not violate third-party rights;
- the contribution does not include unauthorized personal information;
- the contribution does not include real user photos or private service records;
- the contribution follows the repository data policy.

中文说明：
贡献者提交内容时，需要确认贡献内容为原创或有权贡献，不包含未经授权的个人信息，不包含真实用户照片或隐私服务记录。

## Project Use

You allow the project to organize, edit, rewrite, publish, and use your contribution in:

- this open-source repository;
- project documentation;
- templates;
- synthetic or anonymized examples;
- commercial products;
- advanced libraries;
- expert knowledge bases;
- creator education materials.

中文说明：
提交贡献即表示允许项目在开源仓库、文档、模板、商业产品和高级库中整理、改写、使用贡献内容。

## Attribution

The project will try to preserve contributor attribution when practical.

Attribution may be adjusted for documentation clarity, privacy protection, repository maintenance, or aggregated knowledge formats.

中文说明：
项目会尽量保留贡献者署名，但在文档整理、隐私保护、维护和知识聚合场景中，署名形式可能会调整。

## Commercial Collaboration

Open-source contribution does not automatically create a paid commercial relationship, certification relationship, employment relationship, or official partner relationship.

Advanced commercial collaboration, paid expert participation, certified partner cooperation, or model library contribution requires a separate agreement.

中文说明：
高级商业合作、专家共创、认证合作或模型库合作需要另行签约。
