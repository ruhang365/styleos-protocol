# Shopping Keyword Card

This card helps users search for clothing or accessories with direction words.

中文说明：
购物关键词卡用于输出“搜索方向”，不是推荐真实品牌商品。

## Template

```yaml
execution_card_type: "shopping_keyword"
color_direction:
  - ""
silhouette:
  - ""
fabric:
  - ""
scenario:
  - ""
avoid_keywords:
  - ""
```

## Field Notes

- Color direction: near-face color direction, neutral direction, accent color.
- Silhouette: structured, relaxed, straight, soft drape.
- Fabric: light, medium, structured, matte, soft.
- Scenario: work, date, commute, camera, meeting.
- Avoid keywords: high-maintenance, too sheer, overly tight, overly complex, or other user-specific constraints.

## Privacy Note

Do not include real purchase accounts, addresses, or private order screenshots.
