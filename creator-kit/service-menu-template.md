# Service Menu Template

This template helps creators package StyleOS-compatible services.

Prices below are examples only. Creators should adjust based on market, depth, location, expertise, review time, and delivery format.

中文说明：
以下价格仅为示例，不是固定建议。创作者应根据服务深度、专业经验、交付形式和客户群体自行定价。

## Hairstyle Card

For fans who need a focused hairstyle direction.

Suggested scope:

- face-shape and proportion tags;
- current hairstyle constraints;
- 1-3 hairstyle directions;
- avoid list;
- maintenance notes;
- next salon communication card.

Example price range:

- entry: CNY 49-99
- standard: CNY 99-199
- review add-on: CNY 99+

## Color Card

For fans who need color direction for hair, makeup, or outfit accents.

Suggested scope:

- warm / cool / neutral direction;
- contrast direction;
- near-face color suggestions;
- avoid list;
- shopping or styling notes.

Example price range:

- entry: CNY 39-89
- standard: CNY 99-199

## Outfit Card

For fans who need a practical outfit upgrade.

Suggested scope:

- scenario tags;
- body and comfort constraints described safely and respectfully;
- outfit direction;
- 3 outfit formulas;
- avoid list;
- shopping notes.

Example price range:

- entry: CNY 59-129
- standard: CNY 199-399

## Makeup Card

For fans who need daily or camera-ready makeup direction.

Suggested scope:

- makeup direction tags;
- base, eye, lip, and contour priorities;
- low-maintenance version;
- event version;
- avoid list.

Example price range:

- entry: CNY 49-99
- standard: CNY 149-299

## Complete Style Passport

For fans who need an integrated direction across hairstyle, color, outfit, makeup, and scenario.

Suggested scope:

- intake review;
- StyleOS tag set;
- module recommendations;
- full lite report;
- execution checklist;
- optional follow-up review.

Example price range:

- standard: CNY 399-899
- premium: CNY 999+

## 1-on-1 Review

For fans who want manual feedback after receiving a lite report.

Suggested scope:

- private review session;
- answer key questions;
- revise report notes;
- define next action.

Example price range:

- 30 minutes: CNY 199-499
- 60 minutes: CNY 399-999
