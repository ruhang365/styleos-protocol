# Rule Status

Rule status describes where a rule sits in the review lifecycle.

## `starter`

Initial seed rule used for protocol demonstration.

中文说明：
starter 规则是种子内容，用于展示结构和帮助社区共创，不能被包装为专家认证。

## `community-submitted`

Submitted by a contributor or creator, pending quality and privacy review.

## `under-review`

Being reviewed for clarity, non-harmful language, actionability, creator usefulness, execution feasibility, privacy safety, and evidence.

## `expert-reviewed`

Reviewed by qualified experts or experienced practitioners under a defined process.

## `data-supported`

Supported by authorized, anonymized, and responsibly processed data.

## `deprecated`

No longer recommended because it is unclear, low-usefulness, harmful, outdated, or replaced by a better rule.

## `pro-only`

Belongs to a commercial expert library, Pro model library, verified case database, or certified workflow.

## Status Rules

- Public seed content should default to `starter`.
- A rule can be useful while still unverified.
- Status should not be upgraded without review evidence.
- Deprecated rules should keep a short reason when possible.
