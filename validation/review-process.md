# Review Process

The StyleOS review process moves rules from starter content toward reviewed or validated knowledge.

中文说明：
规则从 starter 到 verified 需要经过社区反馈、创作者使用、匿名反馈、专家复核和商业边界判断。

## Flow

```text
Starter rule
  -> community feedback
  -> creator usage
  -> anonymized feedback
  -> expert review
  -> Pro library candidate
  -> public summary or commercial rule
```

## Step 1: Starter Rule

A starter rule demonstrates the protocol structure.

Required:

- status is `starter / unverified`;
- evidence level is usually `E0`;
- limitations are explicit;
- privacy note is included.

## Step 2: Community Feedback

Community members can comment on clarity, harmful language, actionability, and fit with creator workflows.

Feedback must not include real personal data or unauthorized cases.

## Step 3: Creator Usage

Creators may try the rule in private service workflows.

They should record only anonymized, consent-aware feedback summaries.

## Step 4: Anonymized Feedback

Feedback should remove names, photos, contacts, exact dates, addresses, and identifying combinations.

Public examples should be synthetic or fully anonymized.

## Step 5: Expert Review

Experienced practitioners may review the rule for professional usefulness, limitations, and scenario fit.

Expert review should not create absolute claims.

## Step 6: Pro Library Candidate

Rules with strong repeated usefulness may become candidates for a Pro library, commercial expert library, or verified case system.

## Step 7: Public Summary or Commercial Rule

The project may publish a privacy-safe public summary, keep the rule open, deprecate it, or move advanced details to a commercial layer under separate terms.
