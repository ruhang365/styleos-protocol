# Evidence Levels

Evidence levels describe how much support a StyleOS rule has.

中文说明：
证据等级用于防止 starter rules 被误读成专家认证或数据验证结论。

## E0 Synthetic Starter Rule

Protocol demonstration content created from synthetic reasoning.

Current v0.1.2 seed content is mostly E0.

It should be treated as a starting point for community iteration, not as expert-certified advice.

## E1 Community Submitted Rule

A rule submitted by the community, usually based on creator practice or a proposed workflow.

It still requires review for privacy, clarity, safety, and usefulness.

## E2 Repeated Creator Observation

A rule observed repeatedly by one or more creators across similar service situations.

It should include anonymized feedback summaries and clear limitations.

## E3 Expert Reviewed Rule

A rule reviewed by qualified experts or experienced practitioners under a defined review process.

Expert review should not remove the need to state limitations and scenario fit.

## E4 Data-Validated Rule

A rule supported by authorized, anonymized, and responsibly processed data.

It requires clear methodology and privacy safeguards.

## E5 Pro Model Validated Pattern

A rule or pattern validated inside a Pro model or commercial expert system.

This may belong to StyleOS Pro or another commercial layer unless a public summary is intentionally released.

## v0.1.2 Notice

The current seed rules are starter content for protocol demonstration and community iteration. They are not expert-certified recommendations.

中文说明：
当前 v0.1.2 种子规则大部分是 E0，不代表专家认证结论，不代表科学定论，也不代表数据验证结果。
