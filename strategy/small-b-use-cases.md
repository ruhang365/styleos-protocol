# Small-B Use Cases

This document describes how creators and small service businesses may use StyleOS.

## A Fashion Creator Sells Outfit Diagnosis

- User: outfit creator.
- Problem: fans ask for outfit advice, but private consultation is hard to package.
- Workflow: intake form -> outfit tags -> rule card selection -> lite report -> shopping keyword card -> feedback.
- Output: outfit diagnosis report and shopping keywords.
- Business value: turns scattered advice into paid service packages.
- Data feedback: anonymized feedback can improve outfit rules and scenario tags.

## A Hairstylist Offers Hairstyle Suitability Cards

- User: hairstylist or hairstyle creator.
- Problem: clients bring reference images that may not match hair attributes or goals.
- Workflow: fan intake -> face-shape and hair tags -> hairstyle rules -> barber brief -> manual review.
- Output: hairstyle suitability card and barber communication brief.
- Business value: improves salon communication and creates an online service product.
- Data feedback: abstracted barber brief notes can improve hairstyle rules.

## A Makeup Artist Offers Makeup Color Cards

- User: makeup artist.
- Problem: fans ask for product names before color direction is clear.
- Workflow: intake -> makeup taxonomy -> color direction -> makeup rule cards -> makeup artist brief.
- Output: makeup color direction card.
- Business value: creates low-ticket consultation products and upsell paths.
- Data feedback: anonymized feedback can improve daily, professional, and camera makeup rules.

## A Photography Studio Offers Pre-Shoot Styling Advice

- User: photography / styling studio.
- Problem: clients arrive without coordinated outfit, makeup, and hair direction.
- Workflow: shoot scenario -> upper-body priority -> color and outfit rules -> photo shoot brief.
- Output: pre-shoot styling guide.
- Business value: improves shoot preparation and reduces revision cost.
- Data feedback: synthetic shoot briefs and anonymized outcomes can improve camera scenario rules.

## An Image Consultant Offers Full Style Passport

- User: image consultant.
- Problem: full consultation reports are time-consuming and hard to standardize.
- Workflow: intake -> multi-module tags -> selected rule cards -> full lite report -> execution cards.
- Output: style passport covering hairstyle, color, outfit, makeup, accessories, and scenario.
- Business value: supports higher-value consulting packages.
- Data feedback: repeated anonymized patterns can become rule candidates.

## A Career Coach Offers Professional Image Module

- User: career coach.
- Problem: clients need interview or meeting image guidance, but coaches may not have a styling system.
- Workflow: career scenario -> professional image tags -> outfit / makeup / hairstyle rules -> report.
- Output: professional image module inside career coaching.
- Business value: expands coaching services without building a styling system from scratch.
- Data feedback: anonymized professional scenario feedback can improve client meeting and interview rules.
