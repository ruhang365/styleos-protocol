# Makeup Taxonomy

Makeup taxonomy defines starter dimensions for daily, professional, and camera looks.

中文说明：
妆容建议应区分颜色方向和具体产品，不推荐真实品牌或商品。

## Skin Finish

- `skin_finish_natural`
- `skin_finish_clean_matte`
- `skin_finish_soft_glow`
- `skin_finish_camera_ready`

## Eyebrow Direction

- `eyebrow_clean_order`
- `eyebrow_soft_arch`
- `eyebrow_straight_soft`
- `eyebrow_defined`

## Eye Emphasis

- `eye_emphasis_low`
- `eye_emphasis_medium`
- `eye_emphasis_camera`
- `eye_emphasis_soft_definition`

## Cheek Placement

- `cheek_soft_lift`
- `cheek_center_soft`
- `cheek_low_sculpt`
- `cheek_minimal`

## Lip Color Direction

- `lip_color_soft_warm`
- `lip_color_soft_cool`
- `lip_color_neutral_daily`
- `lip_color_polished`

## Clean Look

- `makeup_clean_look`: 气色、眉眼秩序和肤色均匀优先。

## Professional Look

- `makeup_professional_look`: 克制、清晰、稳定，不等于浓妆。

## Camera Look

- `makeup_camera_look`: 考虑灯光吃妆、镜头对比和上镜清晰度。
