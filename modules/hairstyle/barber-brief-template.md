## Example Language

```text
Goal: cleaner and softer workday hairstyle.
Scenario: client-facing workday and profile photo update.
Current concern: side volume feels heavy around the jawline.
Keep: medium length and low maintenance.
Adjust: add soft side layers and reduce weight near the jawline.
Avoid: blunt length ending exactly at the widest jawline point.
Length boundary: keep around collarbone; do not stop at the widest jawline point.
Bangs / parting direction: light side part, no heavy full bangs.
Volume direction: keep light crown support and reduce side heaviness.
Reference keywords: soft face-framing, light layers, workday clean.
Maintenance level: 5-10 minutes daily.
Creator note: This is a synthetic example brief for protocol documentation.
```

## Do-Not-Do

- Do not describe the face or hair as a flaw.
- Do not promise one fixed outcome.
- Do not include private photos, real names, contact details, or salon records in public examples.
- Do not recommend a product brand, medical path, or irreversible change.

## Privacy Note

Do not paste real user photos or private service notes into public repository examples.
