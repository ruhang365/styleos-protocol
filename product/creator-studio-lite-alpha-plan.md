# Creator Studio Lite Alpha Plan

This is a 30-day alpha plan for validating the Creator Launch Kit and future Creator Studio Lite workflow.

中文说明：
该计划用于招募首批创作者内测，验证粉丝咨询标准化、轻量报告生成、规则抽象和付费意愿。

## 30-Day Goals

- 1000 GitHub Stars
- 20 creator alpha users
- 100 test reports generated
- 100 rule cards collected or abstracted
- 10 synthetic or anonymized case studies
- 5 creators willing to pay
- 3 expert / partner leads

## Week 1: Protocol Launch and Creator Recruitment

Goals:

- polish repository README and launch copy;
- publish launch post;
- recruit first creator alpha candidates;
- explain that StyleOS is not a consumer color test;
- direct creators to GitHub Star and Creator Alpha application.

Outputs:

- launch page copy;
- creator recruitment copy;
- social posts;
- alpha user shortlist.

## Week 2: Creator Onboarding and First Report Workflow

Goals:

- onboard selected creators;
- provide intake template and lite report template;
- guide creators through first synthetic or authorized private workflow;
- collect pain points in tagging, rule card selection, and report review.

Outputs:

- first 30-50 test reports;
- workflow friction notes;
- starter rule card feedback.

## Week 3: Feedback and Rule Abstraction

Goals:

- collect feedback from creators;
- abstract repeated creator observations into rule cards;
- separate raw service data from anonymized case summaries;
- identify candidate schema improvements.

Outputs:

- 100 rule cards collected or abstracted;
- 10 synthetic or anonymized case study drafts;
- alpha feedback summary.

## Week 4: Pro Library Boundary and Paid Pilot Design

Goals:

- clarify what should stay open and what belongs to Pro;
- identify creators willing to pay;
- identify expert or partner leads;
- draft paid pilot scope.

Outputs:

- Pro library boundary notes;
- pricing feedback;
- paid pilot candidate list;
- v0.2 hairstyle module priorities.

## Alpha Guardrails

- No raw personal photos in the public repository.
- No real names, contacts, or private fan records in public examples.
- Creator data does not automatically enter the open repository or Pro library.
- Consent, anonymization, and authorization are required before case reuse.
