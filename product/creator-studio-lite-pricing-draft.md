# Creator Studio Lite Pricing Draft

Pricing is exploratory and may change during alpha.

中文说明：
以下只是 early draft，不是正式定价，不构成商业承诺。Alpha 阶段会根据创作者使用反馈调整。

## Creator Free

Suggested scope:

- 5 lite reports / month;
- StyleOS watermark;
- community protocol only;
- basic fan intake template;
- basic report template.

Possible purpose:

- let creators understand the workflow;
- encourage protocol adoption;
- lower trial barrier.

## Creator Starter

Exploratory price:

- 99 RMB / month.

Suggested scope:

- 30 lite reports / month;
- basic templates;
- creator-branded output;
- saved intake history;
- basic feedback collection.

Possible purpose:

- support creators who already have regular fan consultation demand.

## Creator Pro

Exploratory price:

- 299 RMB / month.

Suggested scope:

- 150 reports / month;
- advanced templates;
- profile management;
- partial Pro library access;
- report versioning;
- richer execution cards.

Possible purpose:

- support creators and small-B teams with paid service volume.

## Creator Expert

Exploratory price:

- custom / 999 RMB+ per month.

Suggested scope:

- expert model add-ons;
- certified badge under separate terms;
- advanced Pro library;
- partner execution cards;
- priority review workflow;
- custom service packaging.

Possible purpose:

- support serious creator businesses, image consultants, studios, and expert partners.

## Pricing Boundaries

- Open-source protocol remains Apache-2.0.
- Hosted tools, Pro library access, certification, and expert workflows are commercial services.
- Prices may change during alpha and paid pilot.
- Certification marks and StyleOS brand assets require separate permission.
