## How to Collect Fan Information

Use an intake form to collect:

- target scenario
- current styling concern
- hair or outfit signals
- maintenance willingness
- risk tolerance
- constraints
- goals

Do not ask for more private information than needed.

## How to Create a Lite Report

1. Summarize the user goal.
2. Select relevant tags.
3. Reference 2-5 rule cards.
4. Write 1-2 possible directions.
5. Add maintenance and risk notes.
6. Convert the recommendation into an execution card.
7. Add a disclaimer when evidence level is low.

## How to Submit Abstracted Rules

If you repeatedly observe a useful pattern:

1. Remove personal details.
2. Convert it into conditions, recommendation, avoid notes, scenario, confidence, evidence type, limitations, and privacy note.
3. Use [Rule Card Submission Guide](rule-card-submission-guide.md).
4. Mark it as starter or community-submitted unless reviewed.

## Do Not Publicly Upload Fan Data

Do not submit:

- photos
- names
- contacts
- accounts
- private messages
- service records
- payment records
- identifiable cases

Public examples should be synthetic.
