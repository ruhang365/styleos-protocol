# Governance Lite

StyleOS Protocol uses lightweight governance while the project is early.

## Maintainer Role

Maintainers are responsible for:

- repository direction
- open-core boundary
- privacy safety
- release quality
- merge decisions
- trademark and commercial boundary protection

## Contributor Role

Contributors can submit:

- schema suggestions
- rule cards
- synthetic cases
- documentation improvements
- workflow examples
- evaluation suggestions

## Creator Contributor Role

Creator contributors provide workflow feedback from styling, outfit, hairstyle, makeup, image, photography, or small-B service contexts.

They should abstract patterns without exposing private users.

## Expert Reviewer Role

Expert reviewers may review rule clarity, safety, applicability, and limitations.

Expert review should be documented. It should not erase uncertainty or privacy requirements.

## What Can Be Merged

- clear protocol improvements
- privacy-safe synthetic examples
- starter rule cards with limitations
- schema additions that preserve compatibility
- documentation improvements
- creator workflow examples

## What Goes to Pro Candidate

- advanced expert-reviewed rule sets
- verified case patterns
- commercial workflow logic
- model-assisted scoring systems
- certification criteria
- partner execution systems

Pro candidate content should not be mixed into open seed rules unless intentionally released.

## What Cannot Be Accepted

- real user photos
- real names or contacts
- unauthorized private cases
- scraped third-party content
- harmful or shaming language
- fixed outcome promises
- medical or invasive advice
- trademark misuse
- app scaffolds or dependency additions outside repository scope
