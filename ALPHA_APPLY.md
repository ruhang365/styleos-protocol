# Creator Alpha Application

StyleOS Creator Alpha is a small early test program for creators who want to turn styling experience into structured services.

中文说明：
本轮目标招募 20 位造型 / 穿搭 / 形象 / 发型 / 美妆博主，围绕 v0.2 Hairstyle Module 测试信息采集、规则卡、轻量报告、理发师沟通卡和服务菜单。

## Who Should Apply

- styling creators
- outfit creators
- hairstyle creators
- makeup creators
- image consultants
- photography / styling studios
- small beauty or image-service teams

You are a good fit if you already receive fan or client questions such as:

- What hairstyle direction fits my goal?
- How do I explain this to a hairstylist?
- How can I package a small paid styling service?
- How do I turn repeated advice into reusable templates?

## What Creators Get

- Early access to StyleOS creator workflow documents.
- Hairstyle intake and lite report templates.
- Barber brief card structure.
- Service menu templates.
- Feedback review with the ruhang365 StyleOS team.
- Opportunity to shape future Creator Studio / Cloud direction.

## What Creators Need to Provide

- Feedback on intake forms.
- Feedback on lite report structure.
- Feedback on barber brief usefulness.
- 1-3 synthetic or fully anonymized workflow examples.
- Notes on what makes service delivery easier or harder.

## What Data Should Not Be Submitted Publicly

Do not submit:

- real user photos
- real names
- personal contact details
- private chat logs
- unauthorized creator or client information
- private salon or service records
- payment records
- identifiable case details

Public repo examples must remain synthetic. Private alpha feedback should be authorized and minimized.

## Alpha Timeline

Suggested timeline:

- Week 1: creator onboarding and intake review
- Week 2: hairstyle lite report testing
- Week 3: barber brief and service menu testing
- Week 4: feedback review and roadmap synthesis

## Expected Output

Each creator alpha participant should help validate:

- whether the intake form is easy to use
- whether tags are understandable
- whether rule-card references help explain advice
- whether lite reports are deliverable
- whether barber briefs are actionable
- whether the service menu can support paid pilots

## How ruhang365 Will Use Feedback

ruhang365 will use feedback to improve:

- public schemas
- starter rule clarity
- creator-kit templates
- report structure
- validation checklists
- future hosted Creator Studio / Cloud product design
- Pro library boundary decisions

## Consent and Privacy Note

Creator Alpha feedback should be shared only with consent. Do not include sensitive or identifiable data in public issues, pull requests, or examples.
